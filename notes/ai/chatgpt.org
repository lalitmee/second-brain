*** 1. ELI5 — Explain Like I'm 5

*** 2. TL;DR — Instant Summaries
- Usage: Paste text, then prompt with ~TL;DR~

*** 3. Jargonize — Professional Mode

- Description: Transforms text into a more technical, professional tone — good for LinkedIn posts, pitch decks, and corporate writing. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

*** 4. Humanize — Kill the AI Voice
- Usage: ~Humanize this~
- Description: Makes AI-generated text sound more natural and human, avoiding robotic marketing phrases or generic AI language. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

*** 5. The Feynman Technique — Real Understanding

*** 6. Socratic Method — Interactive Learning

*** 7. Rewrite Like [Specific Person]

*** 8. Inverse Prompt — Reverse Engineering Genius

*** 9. Temperature Control — Creativity Dial

- Description: Adjusts response randomness — high creativity for bold, unexpected ideas; low randomness for precise, focused answers. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
